What is the nutriscore ?

Your products labels understanding could be burdensome ! The nutriscore is a label designed with the help from health-care professionals, it's an easy and quick way to check the safety and the nutritional quality of your food.

the nutriscore is represented by a logo composed of 5 letters (from A to E), associated with a colour scale (from dark green to dark orange).

So, the best nutriscore is specified by the A letter on dark green background.
